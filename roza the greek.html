The Good, The Bad and the Greek

As a person born and raised in Athens, Greece, with a dad born and raised in Canada, I had a lot of northern American influenses growing up. I didn't think I'd miss much moving to Canada myself...
But I did get homesick quicker than expected.

This is a list of what I miss:

  • Being surrounded by history of thousands of years back
  • The weather
  • The sound of cicadas in the summer
  • The amazing food
  • The blue sea
  • My loved ones, Friends and Family

This is a list reason I won't leave Toronto yet:

  • Corrupt Police

    I feel unsafe walking the streets at night. Police many times has proven to be criminally unhelpful.

  • Political corruption and cover-ups.

    Tempi Train Crash
  • Unlivable wages
